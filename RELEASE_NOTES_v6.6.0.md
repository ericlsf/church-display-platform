# v6.6.0 — Alert Rules and Escalation

## Added

- Offline alert delay
- Disk warning and critical thresholds
- Health warning and critical thresholds
- Per-category enable and disable controls
- Quiet-hour suppression for non-critical alerts
- Persistent Alert Rules page

Critical alerts remain active during quiet hours. Disabled categories and
quiet-hour alerts are suppressed from active Alert Center counts without
changing the underlying health data.
