# v3.5.1 — Condensed history and resolved job errors

- History defaults to a compact summary.
- Detailed event and raw health views remain available.
- Pagination and Show more prevent excessively long pages.
- Failed, cancelled, and timed-out jobs can be marked resolved without deleting their history.
- Resolved jobs no longer appear in Dashboard error summaries.
- Jobs page supports individual and bulk resolution.
