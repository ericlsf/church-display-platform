# v4.9.1 — Group actions and maintenance enforcement

## Added

- Saved groups are now selectable Fleet Operations targets.
- Direct displays and group members are merged and deduplicated.
- Maintenance displays are skipped by default.
- Administrators can explicitly override maintenance protection.
- Fleet health shows Maintenance as a separate operational state.
- Maintenance reasons appear directly in Fleet Operations.

## Protected actions

Maintenance protection applies to:

- Sync Now
- Restart Display App
- Software deployment
- Presentation settings
