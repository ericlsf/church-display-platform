Installer integration note

Add the following verification after registration and agent startup:

1. Poll:
   GET /discovery/status?display_id=<stable-id>

2. Display one of:
   - Waiting for approval
   - Approved, waiting for initial playlist
   - Provisioning
   - Ready

3. Do not claim the display is fully ready until:
   - the Hub reports approved;
   - a playlist is assigned;
   - the first set_sync_folder job succeeds;
   - heartbeat reports media_count > 0.

The maintained v4.2 installer already preserves dashed stable IDs. This release
adds readiness and retry support on the Hub and hides the cursor in the player.
