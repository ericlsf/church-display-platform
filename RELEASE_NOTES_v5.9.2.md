# v5.9.2 — Display Settings Page Layout

## Changed

The individual display settings area is reorganized into two rows:

1. `Content & Overlay` occupies a full-width row.
2. `Display Profile` and `Maintenance` share the row beneath it.

## Content editor

With the full page width available, the Content & Overlay section uses:

- settings on the left;
- a larger live preview on the right;
- responsive stacking on narrower screens.

Display Profile and Maintenance become a balanced two-column row and stack on
small screens.
