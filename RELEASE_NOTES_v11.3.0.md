# Church Display Platform v11.3.0

## Live Fleet Status

- Refreshes online state, last-seen age, version, current media, sync state, and screenshots every 15 seconds.
- Pauses polling while the browser tab is hidden.
- Shows a Live/Paused indicator in the fleet header.
- Uses a lightweight JSON endpoint that never queries Google Drive or rclone.
- Preserves selections and open management drawers during refreshes.
